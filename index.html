IRON
— Who We Are

No frills.
No filters.
Just boxing.

Iron District was opened in 2014 by Marcus Webb, a former professional middleweight born and raised on Bermondsey Street. After fifteen years on the circuit, he came home and turned a derelict railway arch into one of South London's most respected boxing gyms.

We're not a fitness trend. We don't sell smoothies. We teach people to box properly — from the first jab to the first fight night.

TRAIN
— What We Offer

Three ways
to step in.

01

Boxing Classes

Structured group sessions for every level — beginners through advanced sparring. Fundamentals, footwork, conditioning, contact.

02

1-to-1 Coaching

Private sessions with our head coaches. Pad work, technical correction, fight prep. Built around you, not a class plan.

03

Competitive Programme

Selected by invitation. Amateur fighters training to compete at ABA and England Boxing level. Ring time. Sparring. Honesty.
