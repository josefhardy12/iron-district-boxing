COACHES
— The People

Coaches.

Every coach at Iron District has fought. Every coach has cornered fighters. We don't hire instructors — we hire boxers who can teach.

WEBB
— Head Coach & Founder

Marcus Webb.

Pro record: 23-4-1 · 14 KO · Middleweight · 1999–2014

Born on Bermondsey Street in 1978. Turned pro at 21 after a decade in the amateur ranks. Fought across the UK and twice in the States. Hung up the gloves in 2014 and opened Iron District the same year, three doors down from his old primary school.

Marcus coaches with the same approach he fought with: technical, patient, brutally honest. He believes every fighter — from first-timer to amateur champion — deserves real attention, not a script.

"This arch was a tyre garage when I was a kid. We've kept the bones the same. The work changes, the place doesn't."
